1.2.3. centos7下可以新建服务service启动

  1. 新建sell.service

  1. vim sell.service
  2. systemctl daemon-reload: 修改文件后，重启配置
  3. systemctl start sell：启动服务
  4. systemctl stop sell: 停止服务
  5. systemctl enable sell: 开机启动
  6. systemctl disable sell: 停止开机启动
